Ingredients

  • 10 Oz ribeye steak
  • Butter
  • Garlic
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Season steak with salt and pepper
  2. Heat pan to medium high heat and coat with oil
  3. Cook steak 5 mins on each side
  4. Baste with butter and garlic
